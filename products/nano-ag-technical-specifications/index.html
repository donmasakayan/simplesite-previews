Nano Ag Technical Specifications

Technical Description

Nano-Ag is a proprietary mixture of complex microbes (algae, bacteria and fungi), enzymes, trace mineral electrolytes, sucrose and kelp. It provides a complete system of microorganism stimulation, accelerating the reproduction rate of beneficial aerobic microorganisms.

Microbial metabolic processes result in increased oxygen in the soil. Nutrients excreted by microbes are like those from earthworms, both are rich topsoil, containing plant available, well-balanced nutrients.

The sucrose provides an energy source for the aerobic activity of soil microbes.

Microbial activity will increase the rate of breakdown of organic and inorganic matter, resulting in improved soil tilth and deliver a high Humic organic matter content and increase nutritional uptake.

Trace mineral electrolytes act as a natural electrolyte and assist plants in their metabolic activities and in pH balancing for improved cellular electrical frequency and plant vigour.

Foliar applications provide colloidal trace minerals or electrolytes, sucrose and kelp. The formulation has a unique ability to signal plant defence responses, resulting in decreased pest pressure. The nutrients are absorbed through the stomata and are immediately available for integration into the plants metabolic processes.


Guaranteed Ingredients

Total Nitrogen (N): 1%
Available Phosphate (P205): 0.1%
Soluble Potash (K20): 5.5%
Other Ingredients: 93.4%

Other Ingredients (93.4%)

Non-plant food material: sucrose, kelp, diafil, dried yeast, Humic acids derived from Humic shale, a proprietary probiotic and enzyme blend. No acid-leaching used.

Microbes

ALGAE: Selected strains, primarily in the blue-green class for oxygenation and soil binding

FUNGI: Selected strains, primarily to produce endo/ectomycorrhizal symbiosis

BACTERIA: Selected strains, including Humic & fulvic absorbers & others selected for their enzyme creation and/or humification abilities

Enzymes

SEA KELP: a slow-growing marine plant is one of nature’s wonder foods and nutrient sources. It’s full of plant growth hormones that contribute to the energy nutrition and strength of plants and humans alike

Mineral Electrolytes

A proprietary blend, selected to give microbes the building blocks for enzymes and cell walls as well as a blend of plant oriented nutrients. Humic & Fulvic Acids, Glucose & Carbohydrates, Lipids, Lignin, Proteins, and Trace Minerals
