What is Nano Ag?

The Nano-Ag is one of the most impactful and cost effective fertilizers on the market. It has organic certification in the United States (OMRI Listed). Nano-Ag provides all the same benefits of a conventional NPK fertilizers, however developed from nanotechnology, its formulae has the following advantages over conventional fertilizers:

  • Restoring soil integrity and bringing soil back to life
  • Restructuring the soil profile, and balancing its pH
  • Promoting microorganism activity in soil
  • Reducing pest pressure and irrigation requirements

It delivers nutrients and conditions the soil

Nano-Ag is an natural fertilizer that is a proprietary blend of 3 components

  1. Microbial stimulators (Algae, Bacteria, Fungi)
  2. Enzymes (Sea kelp)
  3. Mineral electrolytes (Humic & Fulvic Acids, Glucose & and Trace Minerals)

Nano Ag stimulates the microorganisms in the soil. Within 48 hours you have dramatically increased beneficial microorganism activity, that will recondition the soil, convert waste to nutrients and promote optimal pH values.
