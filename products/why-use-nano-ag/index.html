Why Use Nano Ag

It generates optimum results at a low cost.

Farmers consistently see healthier crops, significant yield increases, reduction in irrigation, and a reduction in predatory pests. Nano-Ag provides one of the most cost-effective solutions on the market. Nano-Ag is compatible with other fertilizers and amendments, but for most users, it reduces or even eliminates the need for additional inputs like chemicals and pesticides.


It is Easy -to-apply and transport

Nano-Ag is supplied as a dry powder. Just add fresh (non-chlorinated) water, wait 48 hours to activate then dilute using non chlorinated water (at a ratio of 15: 1) and apply by spraying or soaking seed. No more shipping truckloads of heavy fertilizer and hauling across fields.


It has unbeatable coverage for such a small package.

Nano-Ag is supplied in either 25 gram or 50 gram bottles, or in 2.5 gram or 5 gram sachets.

Although treatments and volumes will vary by crop, farmers can see dramatic results with as little as 2.5 grams of product though results will continue to improve as application rate and frequency increases.


It offers the following benefits:

  • Stimulates plant growth
  • Increase productivity
  • Improve the quality of the soil
  • Nutritional improvement from crops
  • Poison free
  • Crop outputs taste better
  • Crop outputs preserve longer
  • Disease and pest resistance
  • Weed competitiveness
  • Lower input costs
  • Drought resistance
