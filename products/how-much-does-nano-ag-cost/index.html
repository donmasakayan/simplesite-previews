How much does Nano Ag cost

Nano-Ag is a highly concentrated powder. There are available the following packaging options:

  • 50-gram sealed bottle
  • 25-gram sealed bottle (as of July 2020)
  • 5-gram sachet (as of July 2020)
  • 2.5-gram sachet (as of July 2020)

The application rates of Nano-Ag depend on the crop, and the budget of the farmer. The product yields positive impact from small baseline applications, however yield and crop quality will continue to improve with further application.

For reference, applications for rice farming start as low as P750 per hectare with just 2.5 grams of product. Please contact us so we can work with you within your budget to determine the best application protocols and rates.

It is easy-to-apply, with major cost savings compared to the transport of heavy fertilizers.
