How Do I Apply Nano Ag?

Nano-Ag is supplied as a dry powder, and should be kept sealed and in a cool, dry place until activation. The product is first activated, then diluted and applied:

  1. Add approximately 0.5L fresh water (no chlorine) per gram of Nano-Ag, stir and leave in an open container with a loose covering.
  2. After 48 hours, dilute further with non-chlorinated water at a ratio of 15:1, stir thoroughly again.
  3. The Nano-Ag is then ready to apply either for seed soaking, foliar spray or soil spray depending on the chosen application.
  4. Once fully diluted, the Nano-Ag must be applied within a 10-day timeframe after activation.

Treatments and the volume of Nano Ag required will vary for each crop, tree, vegetable or flowering plant (seed, soil, foliar etc). For further information regarding specific treatments, you can contact BioPrime Agri Industries by email (questions.bioprime@gmail.com) or complete the Contact Us form.
