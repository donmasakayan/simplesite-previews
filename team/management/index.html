BioPrime Management Team

BioPrime Agri Industries Inc. is the exclusive distribution and Marketing Company for Nano Ag, in the Philippines and throughout the ASEAN region.

Ben Mead – Chairman

With a career in the banking, industrial and energy sectors, since moving to Asia in 2014 Ben has focused on opportunities in the agricultural sector particularly large-scale plantations of energy crops and other high value species for the energy and other specialty markets. In addition, Ben is the founder of The Mead Foundation which has numerous active initiatives in the Philippines focused on medical missions, livelihood creation the environment.

Mark Quinn – Managing Director

20 years technical and operational experience in the renewable energy industry within the emerging markets, with extensive knowledge in the production of biomass from managed plantations in the Philippines, India and Ghana. Mark was a pioneer in the field of biofuels and instrumental in the market acceptance of biomass as an economically viable fuel for power generation. His experience has involved financing biotechnology and microbiology R&D laboratories in India to evaluate various alternative fuels, such as fast-growing species of trees, Algae and grasses under a controlled experimental environment for 5 years.

Moises Landicho – Field Manager

15 years working experience in agricultural field management and managing contract growing operations for various agricultural crops, across the Philippines. He spent several years in the tobacco industry where he was responsible for achieving a 20% year on year increase in production volumes. Moises’ background in research and training farmers enabled him to develop and implement locally adapted practices in seedling production, crop management and overall good agricultural practices to improve the quality of crops, while ensuring sustainability in every step of the process of the supply chain.
