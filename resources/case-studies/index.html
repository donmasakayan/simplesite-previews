International Case Studies: Pest and Diseases

Army Worms: Observed on grass hay in Alabama, USA which were infesting all surrounding areas accept fields treated with Nano-Ag.

Fusarium: By removing the diseased leaves and treating the soil around the plant is resulting in Fusarium being eliminated. Currently there are ongoing trials using Nano-Ag taking place with key growers in the Philippines.

Rust Disease: Farmers in Kenya were able to completely eliminate this problem using Nano-Ag along with Coffee Berry Disease.

Nematodes: Rose bushes being cultivated in South Africa where not responding to chemical fertilizers to remove Nematodes making the rose bush sick. Nano-Ag treatment resulted in the bush returning to health and productivity.

Aphids: The use of Nano-Ag on alfalfa hay in Nevada, USA resulted in aphids falling of the plants because the pests were not able to consume the healthy plant due to the electrical frequency produced by the Nano-Ag application.
