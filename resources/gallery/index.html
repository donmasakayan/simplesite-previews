INDEPENDENT INBRED AND HYBRID RICE TRIALS

GUIMBA, NUEVA ECIJA, PHILIPPINES (January-April 2020)

RICE VARIETY: Inbred PhilRice (Varieties 160 and 130)

TREATMENT:
12.5 grams of Nano Ag, comprising:
Seed soaking for 12 hours prior to seed-germination (2.5 grams of Nano Ag)
Foliar spraying 20 day prior to transplanting (5 grams of Nano Ag)
Foliar spraying 30 days after transplanting (5 grams of Nano Ag)

YIELD: 9.9 tons per hectare

RICE VARIETY: Hybrid Longping 937

TREATMENT:
12.5 grams of Nano Ag comprising:
Seed soaking for 12 hours prior to germination (2.5 grams of Nano Ag)
Foliar spraying 20 day prior to transplanting (5 grams of Nano Ag)
Foliar spraying 45 days after transplanting (5 grams of Nano Ag)

YIELD: 11.1 tons per hectare

RICE VARIETY: Hybrid SL 8

TREATMENT:
7.5 grams of Nano Ag comprising:
Seed soaking for 12 hours prior to seed germination (2.5 grams of Nano Ag)
Foliar spraying 20 day prior to transplanting (5 grams of Nano Ag)
