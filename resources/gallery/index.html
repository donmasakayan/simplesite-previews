SITE VALIDATION REPORT

Hybrid and Inbred Rice Trials (4 varieties, 2 sites)
Guimba, Nueva Ecija, Luzon, Philippines

Recorded on April 16, 2020: 100 Days after Transplanting

Joselito D. Abesamis, Municipal Agriculturist
Department of Agriculture, Province of Nueva Ecija, Municipality of Guimba

SITE ONE: BUGTONG FARM

Pasong, Inchic, Guimba, Nueva Ecija

RICE VARIETY: Inbred PhilRice 160

TREATMENT:
12.5 grams of Nano Ag, comprising:
Seed soaking for 12 hours prior to seed-germination (2.5 grams of Nano Ag)
Foliar spraying 20 day prior to transplanting (5 grams of Nano Ag)
Foliar spraying 45 days after transplanting (5 grams of Nano Ag)
